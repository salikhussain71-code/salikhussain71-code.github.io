Research

Flagship Project

PAKGOV-RAG

A bilingual Urdu-English Retrieval-Augmented Generation engine for Pakistani government document infrastructure — covering FBR tax circulars, HEC education policy, NADRA identity procedures, SECP corporate filings, and Supreme Court judgments. The system combines hybrid dense + sparse retrieval (FAISS + BM25) with a QLoRA fine-tuned bilingual generation pipeline, evaluated with the RAGAS framework across faithfulness, answer relevance, context precision, and context recall.
